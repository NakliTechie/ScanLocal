How to use

Extract text from images entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

  • 🚀 Fast: PaddleOCR — best for printed text (20 MB)
  • ✍️ Handwriting: TrOCR — accurate on handwriting (500 MB)
  • 📜 Legacy: Tesseract — historic scripts (25 MB)
  • 🧠 Smart: Qwen2-VL — complex docs, tables, forms (1.3 GB)
